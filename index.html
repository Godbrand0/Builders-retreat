I welcome you all to our 2025 Retreat!

Today, we gather to Revitalize, Renew, and Reconnect with God and each other, based on Psalm 51:10-12.

As we begin this journey, I encourage you to come with expectant hearts, ready to encounter God's presence. Let's open ourselves to His leading, allowing Him to rejuvenate our passion, faith, and relationship with Him and reconnect us to His purpose.

During this retreat, let's be sensitive to the Holy Spirit's movement. Expect God to manifest His glory among us. Let's create an atmosphere where He can freely move, touching lives and transforming us.

May this retreat be a turning point for us, where we'll encounter God's love, power, and grace. In the name of Jesus, let's journey together in His presence.

I decree this retreat open in the name of God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.
